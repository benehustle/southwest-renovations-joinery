Kitchens that handle real traffic

SouthWest Renovations & Joinery treats the kitchen as a working factory: hot pans, school bags, and pets all need predictable clearances. We map appliance circuits early, confirm ventilation paths for cooktops, and align bench heights with how tall household members actually prep food. Because we build cabinets in-house, changes to drawer banks or pantry depths happen before sheets are ordered, not after they arrive on the kerb.

From Portland to Tyrendarra, we programme demolition so temporary sinks or BBQ cook-offs are planned where mains water is interrupted. Kitchen renovation FAQs

How do I know if my kitchen project needs a registered building practitioner in Victoria?

Domestic building work over the financial threshold set under Victorian legislation must be carried out by a registered builder and supported by major domestic building insurance where required. During quoting we outline when your scope crosses that threshold and what documentation you should expect before works begin.

Do you handle asbestos checks in older Portland kitchens?

If your home predates the 1990s, we pause strip-out until licensed assessors confirm sheet goods and adhesives. We never disturb suspect materials casually; clearance certificates guide the next demolition stage so everyone on site stays within WorkSafe expectations.

Can we live at home while the kitchen is rebuilt?

Many clients stay put with a temporary dish station in the laundry or garage. We sequence power outages, water isolations, and noisy cutting so you know which nights to plan takeaway, and we secure the site each afternoon if pets roam the house.
