Cabinetry questions

Do custom cabinets meet Australian construction requirements?

We design and install joinery to align with the National Construction Code (NCC) referenced in Victoria, including structural fixing where units are tall or support stone tops, and moisture-sensible details adjacent to wet areas. For major domestic building work, your contract should reference the applicable Victorian domestic building requirements—ask us during quoting if your scope triggers additional compliance steps.

How long does a full robe wall take around Portland?

Lead times move with material availability and workshop load, but most robe walls move from approved drawings to install inside a few weeks. Rural deliveries beyond Portland may add a day for travel—we programme that up front so you are not left guessing.

Can you match existing skirting and architraves in older homes?

Yes. We take moulding samples or detailed photos, machine matching profiles where timber is still available, and prefinish in controlled conditions so the colour reads consistently against heritage trims common across south-west weatherboards.
