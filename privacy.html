What we collect

When you phone, email, or use the contact form, we collect the details you choose to provide—usually your name, phone number, email address, property address, and project notes. The website form does not store submissions in a database; it is a browser-based message until our office records it in our job diary or email system.

How we use information

We use your contact details to respond to quote requests, schedule site visits, and deliver contract paperwork required for domestic building work in Victoria. We do not sell mailing lists. If you become a client, some details may appear on invoices, variation notices, or insurance paperwork as required by law.

Storage and security

Office records are stored securely on password-protected systems and in locked filing cabinets where hard copies are required. Email is provided by our business host; follow sensible precautions when sending sensitive documents and call us if you prefer encrypted transfer.

Your rights

Under the Australian Privacy Principles you may request access to the personal information we hold or ask for corrections. Contact admin@swreno.au or phone 0429 610 525 and we will respond within a reasonable period. If you have concerns you cannot resolve with us, you may contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner.

Questions about privacy?

Call or email and we will explain how your data is handled for your specific project.
