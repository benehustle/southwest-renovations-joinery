Our story on Barkly Street

We are a family-owned and operated business focused on giving Portland and South West Victoria a premium, low-drama renovation experience. The crew carries more than forty years of combined industry time, and when we acquired the business in 2025 we kept the vans rolling with one clear brief: pair in-house custom cabinetry with complete kitchen, bathroom, and home renovations or extensions that respect how regional homes actually live.

We grew tired of hearing about radio-silent builders, so we built a rhythm of written updates, photo checkpoints, and decisions captured in writing before materials are ordered. That means fewer variations, fewer surprises, and a handover where the silicone lines, door margins, and hardware alignments are checked in daylight. Whether you are modernising a weatherboard cottage or expanding for multigenerational living, we programme trades to minimise dust through bedrooms, protect existing floors, and keep pets safe.

Our workshop mindset treats every cabinet run as part of a wider system—plumbing rough-ins, ventilation, lighting layers, and future maintenance access—so you are not chasing defects six months later. If you want a team that answers the phone, shows up when agreed, and finishes with a clean site, we are ready to walk your rooms and map the next step.
