SouthWest Renovations & Joinery is a family-owned and operated business delivering Portland and South West Victoria a calm, well-run renovation path. Our team brings over forty years of combined trade experience, and we acquired the business in 2025 with a simple focus: transparent communication, accurate cabinetry built in-house, and kitchens, bathrooms, and extensions finished to a high standard.

We know unclear updates derail projects, so we keep you informed at each stage and coordinate trades to protect your floors, pets, and routine. Whether you need a full kitchen overhaul or a tailored wardrobe wall, we measure twice, programme works sensibly, and hand over a space that feels settled on day one.
