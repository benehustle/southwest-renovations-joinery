Choose the right starting point

Some projects are cabinetry-led, some are full room rebuilds, and some reshape how the whole property works. We keep joinery, carpentry, and finishing coordination under one roof so you are not left stitching the programme together yourself.

Storage or fit-out first

Start here if the room layout mostly works and the biggest win is better cabinetry, robes, pantry storage, or a custom vanity.

Single-room transformation

Choose a kitchen or bathroom renovation when plumbing, electrical, lighting, and joinery all need to be rebuilt in one coordinated package.

Bigger structural change

Whole-home renovations, extensions, decks, and outdoor works suit projects that affect circulation, structure, or how the house opens up.

What stays with one lead

You get one contact across quoting, site supervision, joinery coordination, and sequencing. That makes decision-making faster when appliances shift, existing walls are not square, or weather delays an outdoor stage.

  • One programme instead of trade-by-trade juggling.
  • Clear advice on whether your job is joinery-led, room-led, or structural.
  • Practical staging so the house stays usable where possible.
